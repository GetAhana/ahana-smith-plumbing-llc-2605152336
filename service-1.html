Repairs

Leak detection & plumbing repairs you can trust

Leaks range from slow drips that waste water to burst pipes that flood your home in minutes. We locate the source quickly, present honest repair-or-replace options with firm pricing, and pressure-test every connection before you pay.

Repair standards that protect your home

  • Pressure testing at 80 PSI to verify repair integrity before we leave
  • Video inspection for stubborn leaks using sewer camera when needed
  • Code-compliant materials and joints for Arizona residential standards
  • One-year labor warranty on all repair work and installations

Hundreds of Phoenix-area homes and businesses have trusted us for leak detection, faucet repair, and full repipe work since 2012.

How we handle Repairs

Our three-step leak repair process keeps you informed and confident every step of the way.

  • We diagnose the leak — You describe where you noticed the problem—water pooling, a wet spot, or a spike in your bill. We inspect the visible pipes, check under fixtures, and use water meters or camera inspection to pinpoint hidden leaks.
  • You choose repair or replace — We explain your options honestly: a quick seal for a minor drip, a new valve or connection, or a full section repipe if the line is compromised. You get firm pricing upfront so there are no surprises.
  • We test and document — After the repair, we pressure-test at 80 PSI to confirm the fix, take photos of the finished work, and walk you through the warranty coverage. We clean up completely before we leave.
When to call us

Watch for these signs of a hidden leak or plumbing problem that needs attention soon.

  • Water stains or soft spots on ceilings, walls, or under cabinets
  • Rising water bill with no change in usage
  • Low water pressure in one or multiple fixtures
  • Rust-colored water or metallic taste
  • Mold or musty smell near walls, under sinks, or in crawl spaces
