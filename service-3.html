Drains

Drain cleaning & clog removal in Phoenix

Slow drains and backups are frustrating—and they signal trouble beneath the surface. We cable or hydro-jet clogs away, then inspect with a sewer camera to identify the root cause and prevent it from happening again.

Drain-clearing methods for every situation

  • Snake cable for localized clogs in sink, tub, and branch lines
  • Hydro-jetting at 4000 PSI for stubborn buildup and tree-root intrusion
  • HD sewer camera inspection to diagnose offsets, bellies, and damage
  • Written findings and prevention tips so the clog doesn't return quickly

We've cleared thousands of drains across Phoenix and surrounding towns—from slow kitchen sinks to main-line backups that need urgent attention.

How we handle Drains

Our drain-clearing process finds the problem, fixes it, and teaches you how to keep it from coming back.

  • We locate the blockage — You tell us which drains are slow or backed up. We assess whether it's a branch line (one sink or fixture) or the main line (multiple fixtures). Then we feed a cable or hydro-jet line to clear the clog.
  • We clear and inspect — Once the clog is out, water should drain normally. We use our sewer camera to look inside the line and identify what caused the blockage—tree roots, grease buildup, offset joints, or broken pipe.
  • You get a report and prevention tips — We show you the camera video, explain what we found, and give you written findings. We coach you on how to keep that drain clear—what to avoid putting down, and when a follow-up inspection makes sense.
When to call us

These warning signs often mean a drain or main line needs professional cleaning.

  • Water drains slowly from sinks, tubs, or showers
  • Sewage smell coming from drains or yard
  • Multiple drains backing up at the same time
  • Gurgling sounds from toilets or drains
  • Soft spots in the yard or patches of extra-green grass
